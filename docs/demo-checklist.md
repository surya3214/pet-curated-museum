# Demo Checklist

## Before recording
- Backend runs locally
- Frontend runs locally
- GEMINI_API_KEY is set
- Sample pet photo is ready
- 3 to 5 day photos are ready

## Demo flow
1. Show upload screen
2. Upload pet photo
3. Upload day photos
4. Generate exhibit
5. Show title, subtitle, and artifact cards
6. Play narration
7. Show reset flow

## Things to avoid
- Slow image selection
- Weak pet persona image
- Too many similar day photos
- Browser audio muted
